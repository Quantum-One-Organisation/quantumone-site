Frequently asked questions

What is QuantumOne?+
QuantumOne is a personal optimisation engine. It is a health and self-optimisation app that brings every signal you already collect (wearables, blood tests, genetics, DEXA, CGM, supplements, biohacks, nutrition and environment) into one place, cross-references it against the latest research, and turns it into prioritised, actionable advice.
What is a personal optimisation engine?+
A personal optimisation engine connects your health and lifestyle data sources, runs sophisticated correlations across them, checks the patterns against published research, and outputs specific “do this next” actions with expected impacts. So you optimise based on your own data, instead of generic advice based on population averages.
How does QuantumOne work?+
In three steps. You connect what you already run: devices, supplements, biohacks, blood tests, genetics, and other sources. The engine cross-references your data with the latest research, finding correlations and optimisations nothing else can. Then it gives you a prioritised list of “do this next” actions, each with an expected impact.
How is QuantumOne different from Oura, Whoop, or Apple Health?+
Wearable apps are excellent at collecting and charting their own data, but each lives in its own silo and leaves interpretation to you. QuantumOne is the layer above them: it connects your wearables with your blood work, genetics, supplements, biohacks and environment, finds the correlations across all of it, and tells you what to do about them.
What data sources are supported?+
Wearables and sleep tech (Oura, Apple Watch, Whoop, Eight Sleep, Fitbit, Garmin, and more), blood test PDFs, raw genetic files, DEXA scans, CGM data, supplements and nootropics, biohacks, nutrition, measurements, self-reported surveys, and environmental data like air quality, mould, pollen and tap water, plus quite a lot more.
Can QuantumOne tell me if my supplements are working?+
Yes, that is one of its core jobs. QuantumOne correlates your supplement and dosing logs against your genetics, sleep, blood markers, and other data, then shows which supplements are linked to benefits and which show zero effect. You can refine your stack and stop paying for what isn't working.
Is my health data private?+
Yes. QuantumOne is anonymous by design. No name or address is stored, any geolocation data (used for weather, air quality, pollen, etc) is only accurate to ~15 miles. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit, and your account is locked to your device with biometric security.
Is my genetic data safe?+
Your raw genetic file is used for one purpose: personalising your correlations and advice. Like everything else in QuantumOne, it is anonymous by design, encrypted at rest and in transit.
Does QuantumOne give medical advice?+
No. QuantumOne is a general wellness and self-optimization tool, not a medical device. Correlations and advice are statistical estimates, not clinical predictions, and it does not provide medical advice, diagnosis or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making health decisions.
Do I need to buy new wearables?+
No. QuantumOne works with whatever you already run. You can keep your existing wearables, lab work, supps, and biohacks. Nothing new is required to start. But QuantumOne will tell you what can be removed or added.
Is there a free trial?+
Yes. Every account starts with a one-month free trial with full access to the whole engine. You can cancel anytime, and cancelling is designed to be as easy as signing up.
